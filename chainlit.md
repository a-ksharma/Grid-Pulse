# Welcome to Grid-Pulse! 👋

Grid-Pulse is your personal AI-powered expense tracker. Just chat naturally — no forms, no spreadsheets, just conversation.

Your data is private and tied to your Google account, so everything you add is only visible to you.

---

## What can I help you with?

**Tracking expenses:**
- *"Add ₹500 for groceries today"*
- *"I spent ₹1200 on a Uber ride yesterday"*
- *"Add an expense of ₹350 for lunch at a cafe on 28-03-2026"*

**Reviewing your spending:**
- *"Show my expenses for this month"*
- *"List everything I spent on food in March"*
- *"What did I spend between 1st and 15th April?"*

**Summarizing & insights:**
- *"Summarize my expenses for this month"*
- *"How much did I spend on transport in March?"*
- *"Which category did I spend the most on this week?"*

**General questions:**
- I can also answer everyday questions beyond expenses — just ask!

---

## Tips

- Dates are understood naturally — you can say *"last Monday"*, *"yesterday"*, or *"15-03-2026"*
- All amounts are tracked in **INR (₹)**
- You can filter summaries by category for a focused view
- When a tool is being used, you'll see it appear as a step below the message
